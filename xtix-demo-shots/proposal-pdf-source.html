What XTIX does

A summary of what XTIX puts in place for the three-venue group operation.

One-screen embedded checkout on your sites
Tickets sold directly on duplex.cz, rooftopclub.cz and moonclub.cz — your branding, your event pages, every ticket type the group sells today (Standard, VIP, VIP Table, VIP Terrace, Bottle Service, Lounge) on a single screen.
Multi-currency at checkout
A range of currencies the buyer can select at the moment of purchase — tailored to international tourist traffic.
16+ languages selectable at checkout
The buyer reads the entire flow in their own language — covering every market that lands in Prague on a weekend.
Apple Pay + Google Pay one-tap
The single biggest conversion lift on mobile, where most international ticket buyers actually buy.
Master account with per-venue sub-accounts
DupleX, Rooftop and Moon teams each get their own access. You get one master view across all three — one back-office to run the group.
Group-level CRM under that master account
Every guest tagged at the booking moment: country, language, VIP tier, table preference, bottle service history, theme-night preference, cross-venue visits, lifetime spend. The Friday-DupleX → Saturday-Rooftop → Sunday-Moon international tourist becomes one returning guest across all three venues.
Free AI helper tool built in
Drafts marketing copy, ticket descriptions, event announcements and buyer messages in the group's voice, in any language you need.
Pre-event campaigns to ticket buyers
Request through the system — our team sends the campaign from your name to every buyer of any specific event (lineup updates, dress code, table confirmations, last-call mailings).
Full promo code engine
Straight promo codes, presale codes, member-brings-member referral codes — DupleX VIP regulars get the Rooftop priority code, the Friday Madness regular gets the Moon Full Moon presale, the influencer gets a tracked referral link.
Customer service for ticket buyers handled by XTIX
Every ticket question, refund request, technical issue at checkout, lost-ticket reissue — comes through us, not your venues. Removes the full buyer-support workload from the team.
Ticket scanning + live event analytics
Every scan time-stamped, real-time arrival graph during the night, peak arrival windows by event type, late-arrival patterns by VIP tier, queue-management data — turns each night into actionable data.
